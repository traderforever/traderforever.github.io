Design Principles

White Space

Xbox

www.xbox.com

This webpage from microsoft xbox isn't afraid to have whitespace.  There is a clean simplistic feeling to this page that doesn't strain the eye and the eye is naturally drawn to the main xbox news about their next generation consoles.

Contrast

Trading View

www.tradingview.com 

The contrast on this website is pleasant for me.  The almost black background with white text makes reading easy.  Also the colors of blue for the charts against the dark background catch the eye.  The reds and greens make it easy to know what prices have gone up and what are going down.

Repetition

Amazon

www.amazon.com

Amazon's site has a good example of repetition.  They are showing each of their products in a repetitive grid format.  This makes it easy for the user to scroll down the images to see what they are looking for.
