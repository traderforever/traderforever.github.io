Tours and City Knowledge

Places you need to go

To make the most of your time one the street of Cozumel pick up our out unique map of the 10 recommended stops that you need to make while you cruise around town.

If you plan your day just right, you can hit up all the temples in one day, but we recommend taking two days as there are a lot of fun roads and goodies on the northern part of the island.

You can feel safe to take out your camera and snap photos in any tourist location and along any of the main roads. For the most part people are very friendly and will help you with any pictures you may need and directions.


A brief History

Cozumel was a commercial and ceremonial centre during the Mayan period (about 250 to 950 ce), and the island has several small archaeological sites. In 1518 Juan de Grijalva led the first Spanish expedition to Cozumel. In 1519 Hernán Cortés used the island as a base for Spanish efforts to conquer the Maya.
